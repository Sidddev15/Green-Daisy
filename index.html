    Our Teas

    Take your taste buds on a journey with our exotic tea blends, featuring unique and unexpected flavor combinations that will delight and surprise.

    Green Tea (Whole Leaf)

    The tea is hand-picked from the lush tea gardens in Golaghat, Assam which are known for their ideal climate and fertile soil.

    Organic Green Tea

    Our handmade whole-leaf organic green tea from Karbi Anglong, Assam is a rare and exquisite tea that is carefully crafted using traditional methods.

    Black Tea (HandMade)

    Our tea is of the highest grade, with a rich, full-bodied flavor and a smooth finish. Made from whole leaves that are carefully selected and processed by hand, our black tea retains its natural flavors and aromas, while also being rich in antioxidants and other health benefits.

    Black Tea (Machine Made)

    Grown on a small tea estate, this tea is carefully handpicked and processed using traditional methods that have been passed down through generations.

    Darjeeling Green Tea

    The tea has numerous health benefits, including boosting metabolism, promoting weight loss, reducing stress, and improving cognitive function. It has a unique taste that is cherished by tea lovers worldwide, making it one of the most sought-after teas in the world.

    Darjeeling Black Tea (Premium Grade)

    A premium-grade tea that is hand-plucked during the summer season in Darjeeling, India. The tea has numerous health benefits, including promoting heart health, boosting metabolism, and reducing the risk of certain cancers.
