    Discover the Story Behind Green Daisy: Our Passion for Quality Tea

    Introducing Green Daisy, the luxury tea brand that brings the rich, authentic taste of India to your cup. Our teas are hand-picked from the finest tea gardens from the tea capital of the world - Assam & Darjeeling whose fertile soil and ideal weather conditions produce the most exquisite tea leaves.

    Tea picking is a delicate process that requires great skill and attention to detail. At our tea plantation in India, we ensure that only the finest tea leaves are selected for processing. These leaves are hand-picked by our experienced tea pickers, who carefully choose only the freshest and most tender leaves.

    Once the leaves have been picked, they undergo a meticulous processing procedure. First, the leaves are withered to remove excess moisture. Then, they are rolled to release the natural flavors and aromas. The leaves are then allowed to ferment, which gives the tea its distinctive taste and aroma. During the processing, it is crucial to maintain the appropriate temperature, which varies depending on the type of tea. The quality of our tea is of the utmost importance to us. We use only the freshest and highest quality tea leaves, which are carefully processed to preserve their natural goodness.

    Green Tea (Whole Leaf)

    The tea is hand-picked from the lush tea gardens in Golaghat, Assam which are known for their ideal climate and fertile soil. Unlike mass-produced teas, our green tea is made using traditional methods, with each leaf carefully selected and processed by hand to preserve its natural flavors and aromas.The result is a tea that is not only delicious but also rich in antioxidants and other health benefits.

    Organic Green Tea (Whole Leaf)

    Our handmade whole-leaf organic green tea from Karbi Anglong, Assam is a rare and exquisite tea that is carefully crafted using traditional methods. The tea is hand-picked from the remote tea gardens in Karbi Anglong, which are nestled in the hills and known for their unique terroir and pristine environment. Our green tea is made from whole leaves that are carefully selected and processed by hand, using time-honored techniques that have been passed down through generations.

    Black Tea (HandMade)

    The tea is hand-picked from the lush tea gardens in Golaghat, which are known for their ideal climate and fertile soil. TGFOP, or Tippy Golden Flowery Orange Pekoe, is a grading term used to describe the quality of tea leaves. Our tea is of the highest grade, with a rich, full-bodied flavor and a smooth finish. Made from whole leaves that are carefully selected and processed by hand, our black tea retains its natural flavors and aromas, while also being rich in antioxidants and other health benefits.

    Black Tea (Machine Made)

    Grown on a small tea estate, this tea is carefully handpicked and processed using traditional methods that have been passed down through generations. The result is a tea that is full-bodied and rich, with a smooth, malty flavor and a hint of sweetness. This tea is made from the finest, organic tea leaves, which are grown without the use of any harmful chemicals or pesticides. With its deep, amber color and delightful aroma, this tea is a true pleasure to savor.

    Darjeeling Black Tea

    A premium-grade tea that is hand-picked during the spring season in Darjeeling, India. "Finest Tippy Golden Flowery Orange Pekoe 1", indicating that it is made up of only the finest quality tea leaves. This tea has a delicate floral aroma and a musky spiciness, with a light golden color. The tea has numerous health benefits, including boosting metabolism, promoting weight loss, reducing stress, and improving cognitive function. It has a unique taste that is cherished by tea lovers worldwide, making it one of the most sought-after teas in the world.

    Darjeeling Black Tea (Premium Grade)

    A premium-grade tea that is hand-plucked during the summer season in Darjeeling, India.This tea has a robust and full-bodied flavor, with a distinctive musky spiciness and a sweet aroma. It is rich in antioxidants and has moderate caffeine content, making it an ideal beverage to help kick-start your day.
